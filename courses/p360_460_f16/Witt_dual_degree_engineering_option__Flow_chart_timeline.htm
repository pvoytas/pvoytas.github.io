Witt dual-degree engineering option: Flow chart/timeline

Every year:

Before classes start, contact the dual-degree liaison (presently Dr. Jeremiah Williams) to let him know that this is a possible plan. If you are interested in 3-2 (as opposed to 4-2) scheduling is very tight and unless you tranferred in many credits, you must start your very first semester.

Pre-req's do vary from school to school and can vary from one program to the next so

  • start planning early
  • explore what fields of engineering you might be interested in and find the pre-req's

By Fall of Jr year:

If you will be pursuing 3-2, you will usually apply in fall of your Jr. year so...

Have an idea of what engineering program you'd like to study and know the application deadlines (typically Fall for following fall).

If you will be applying for financial aid at the engineering school, know the deadlines for that.
