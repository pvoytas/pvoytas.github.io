Resume'

  • Witt: Developing a Resume
  • Purdue Online Writing Lab (resume workshop)
  • Avoid the Top 10 Resume Mistakes  (google "resume mistakes" to find many, often conflicting, versions)
  • Google HR top Resume mistakes
  • Tip on resume's: you may well want more than one version if applying to more than one type of place--customization can improve your chances.
  • Tips on references:
    • Ask your references if they are willing before you give their name to someone (generally)
    • Let your references know that you are making specific applications
    • If you are asking that they write a letter of reference, give them as much information as possible about the position, about what you think your relevant experience may be
